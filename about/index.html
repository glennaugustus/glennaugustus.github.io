Friends, Family and Future Finkin

I’m Glenn, a passionate advocate for the power of storytelling and the beauty of simplicity in technology. Your visit here is appreciated. With a background (and foreground) in enterprise technology and a love for writing, I strive to create content that resonates as not only to fellow technologists but to anyone who is technocurious to bring subjects that can seem distant withn reach.

I believe in the importance of clarity when the world seems obscure, and the joy of sharing experiences as a way to help others, whether related to technology or meeting some of life’s challenges.

I work for DXC Technology as a Technologist, you can find more information about my work on LinkedIn.

What is a Technology Adventurer? For me this is the reason I got into technology in the first place, it represents how to survive the constant and violent changes that are always underfoot with the need to adapt and evolve, whether it’s best thought of as riding waves, or jumping chasms, it’s about having the skills and aptitude to build, rent or buy the vehicle that can take others there with you, and to not forget your shovel and paintbrush as the road is seldom finished and you need to leave signposts!

And AI-Human Integrator? Sounds a bit generalist? Well that is exactly what it is, a value and impact orientation to this latest disruptive era, navigating the complex landscape of AI and its integration into our lives, bridging gap between technology and us as humanity. I believe in the massive potential of AI to enhance our lives, but employ the recognition and experience to help understand the importance of ethical, legal, security and social dimensions that frame responsible usage.

My writing often includes my personal journey, exploring themes of entertainment, family, and the human experience and therefore reflect none other than my own opinion, not the opinions of my employer or any other organization, unless it is explicitly stated.

When I’m not writing or designing systems using AI (though the convergence is palpable), you can find me exploring the outdoors, enjoying a good book, film or more recently my wood fired pizza oven, and of course, spending time with friends and family.
